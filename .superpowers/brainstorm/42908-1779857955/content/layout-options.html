B · Photoshop / After Effects

Big preview left, right rail stacks Layers (flat list of subject+behavior combos) and Properties of the selected one. No tree — each row is one effect on one subject.

Strength: Familiar to creators from AE/CapCut. Flat = no nesting confusion. Weakness: Many behaviors on one subject get visually noisy.

C · Preview-First Dock

Preview dominates. Bottom dock shows horizontal "subject → behavior" chips like a sentence. Right rail is purely properties of whatever's selected. Direct: click the subject in the preview to select it.

Strength: Subjects feel tangible — you click them in the preview. Casual, modern. Weakness: Less efficient for complex stacks; bottom dock scrolls horizontally.
